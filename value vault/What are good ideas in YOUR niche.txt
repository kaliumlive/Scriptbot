What are good ideas in YOUR niche?

We can of course pull some good ideas from top creators and tools like chat GPT…

But to really know what a good idea for YOU is, we need to look into your TARGET AUDIENCE and put ourselves in their position - anticipate what they want to hear

So, go ahead and put this prompt into Chat GPT

<aside>

> **“I am doing market research on my social media target audience and I am a ___**
> 

> **give me 20 common pain points in my niche**
> 

> **give me 20 common questions in my niche**
> 

> **give me 20 common misconceptions in my niche**
> 

> **Put this all into a chart”**
> 
</aside>

Every all 60 answers AI gives you can be a content idea and also help frame the value that you give because you will know what your audience really wants to hear
