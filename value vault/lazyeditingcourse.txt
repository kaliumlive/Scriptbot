# Lazy Editing Masterclass - Lazy Lakshay

This document summarizes the core principles and roadmap from the "Editing Masterclass" by Lazy Lakshay.

## 1. The Editing Roadmap
- **Toddler Level (5-7 Days)**: Basic cleanup, removing dead air, simple cuts, static images.
- **Beginner Level (15-30 Days)**: Storytelling context, hiding jump cuts with B-roll, basic text overlays, background music layering.
- **Intermediate Level (3-9 Months)**: **Audience Psychology**. Hook in first 5-7 seconds, motion graphics for retention, color grading for mood.
- **Pro Level (1 Year+)**: 3D visual storytelling, camera animations (keyframing), consistent branding, "Taste" development.

## 2. Cinematic & Engagement Principles
- **The Hook (5-7 Seconds)**: Critical window. High-intensity visuals and fast-paced editing.
- **Sound Resets**: Sound is 50% of the video. Use "Whoosh", "Risers", "Base Drops" as visual resets every few seconds.
- **Storytelling over Packaging**: Narrative structure and emotion are more important than fancy graphics.
- **Invisible Transitions**:
    - **Hiding Jump Cuts**: B-roll or digital zooms.
    - **Match Cuts**: Visual connection through overlapping shapes/movements.
    - **Camera Animations**: Digital camera movement via keyframing.
- **Visual Pacing**: Rhythm dictated by narrative flow and audience psychology.

## 3. Key Roles in Modern Editing
The most in-demand role is the **Mixed Media Editor**, combining story, motion, audio, color, and VFX.

## 4. AI for Efficiency
- Use AI (LLMs) for brainstorming/strategy.
- Use AI generation for B-roll and visual assets to work 10-20x faster.
- Maintain **Artistic Taste** as the primary competitive advantage.
