Title: How to Go Pro in 90 Minutes (Professional Editing Framework)

Goal: Break into pro-level editing (broadcast TV commercial quality).

Key Techniques:
1. Comedy Editing
   - Timing is everything.
   - Using contrast to land jokes.

2. Documentary Editing
   - Building a narrative from raw footage.
   - Pacing and rhythm.

3. Cross Cutting
   - Managing multiple timelines or perspectives.
   - Building tension and suspense.

4. Contrast & Emotion
   - Using visual and sonic contrast to evoke specific feelings.
   - Adding drama to any edit.

5. Building Tension/Suspense
   - Forest fire analogy: The danger is immediate when smoke and flames are shown with urgency.
   - Keep the audience on the edge of their seats by managing visual information reveals.

Hands-on approach: Use real exercises to master these techniques.
Dynamic & Suspenseful scenes: The goal is to make any edit feel intense and dynamic.
