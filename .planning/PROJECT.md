# Scriptbot

## Mission
To serve as the **Autonomous One-Man Content Agency** for solopreneurs. Scriptbot replaces the need for a hired content team by automating ideation, scriptwriting (AV Storyboarding), and multi-platform distribution while providing a single source of truth for engagement tracking.

## Vision
A world where a single creator can compete with full media agencies. Scriptbot turns a raw idea into a high-production-value video through an agentic swarm that handles:
- **Ideation**: Fleshing out or brainstorming viral concepts.
- **Storyboarding**: Designing frame-by-frame audio-visual cues (mographs, movie scenes, B-roll).
- **Publishing**: 1-click verified distribution.
- **Insights**: An unified dashboard for tracking "likes" and growth across the social graph.

## Core Value Proposition
- **Agentic Collaboration**: Not just a tool, but a partner that iterates on your scripts.
- **AV Storyboarder**: Interactive, frame-by-frame direction for mographs, stock/movie footage, and B-roll.
- **Consolidated Tracking**: One dashboard for Instagram, YouTube, and LinkedIn growth.

## Technical Identity
- **Stack**: Next.js 16 (App Router), Supabase, AI SDK (Gemini/Groq), FFmpeg/Puppeteer (Video Processing).
- **Architecture**: Decoupled agentic pipelines (Trend Scout, Storyboarder, Publisher).
- **Deployment**: Vercel + Supabase (ap-south-1).
